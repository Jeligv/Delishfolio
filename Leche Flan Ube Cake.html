Leche Flan Ube Cake

Ube cake flan is your favorite ube cake with leche flan on top. And yes, it‚Äôs as delicious as it sounds! Easy to make too. Perfect for the holidays and all your celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • Condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Cream of tartar
  • Ube extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Caramel: In a small saucepan over medium heat, pour 1/2 cup of sugar (optional) and melt it until it turns golden brown. Quickly pour the caramel into the bottom of a cake pan or llanera and swirl to coat evenly. Set aside to harden.
  2. Make the Leche Flan Layer: In a mixing bowl, combine: 4 egg yolks 1 can (300ml) condensed milk 1 can (250ml) evaporated milk 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional) Beat gently until smooth, making sure not to create too many bubbles. Pour this mixture over the set caramel in your cake pan.
  3. Prepare the Ube Cake Batter: Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). In a separate bowl, combine: 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 tsp baking powder 1/4 tsp salt In another bowl, beat together: 3 eggs 1/2 cup sugar 1/4 cup oil 1 tsp cream of tartar 1/2 cup milk 2-3 tsp ube extract (adjust for desired color and flavor) Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until smooth.
  4. Assemble the Cake: Carefully pour the ube cake batter over the leche flan layer. Do not stir.
  5. Bake the Cake: Cover the cake pan with aluminum foil. Place the cake pan in a larger baking dish filled with about 1 inch of hot water to create a water bath (bain-marie). Bake for 45‚Äì60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the cake layer comes out clean.
  6. Cool and Serve: Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results. To serve, carefully run a knife around the edges, then invert the cake onto a serving plate. The caramel will flow over the cake, giving it a beautiful topping.
