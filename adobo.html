Chicken Adobo

A Filipino comfort dish simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Peppercorns

Instructions:

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves in a pot.
  2. Add chicken and simmer for 30 minutes until tender.
  3. Serve with rice and enjoy!
