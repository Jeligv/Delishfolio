Chocolate Brownies

Dense, fudgy, and irresistibly chocolatey dessert bars.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°C and grease a pan.
  2. Mix butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in cocoa and flour until smooth, bake for 25 minutes.
