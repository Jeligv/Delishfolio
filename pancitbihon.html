Pancit Bihon

Stir-fried rice noodles with vegetables and meat, classic Filipino dish.

Ingredients:

  • 200g bihon (rice noodles)
  • 1 cup sliced vegetables (carrot, cabbage, bell pepper)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken/pork
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Soak noodles in water until soft. Drain.
  2. Saut√© meat and vegetables. Add noodles.
  3. Season with soy sauce and stir-fry for 3-5 min.
