Bicole Express

Bicol Express is a traditional Filipino dish, composed of pork that has been simmered in coconut milk. This dish has a creamy, savory, and spicy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Pork belly.
  • Coconut milk
  • Coconut cream
  • Shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • Garlic
  • Chili pepper
  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Serrano pepper
  • Maggi Magic Sarap

Instructions:

  1. Proceed by combining minced ginger, crushed garlic, chopped onion, sliced pork, chopped chili pepper, and coconut milk. Mix everything together before turning-on the heat.
  2. Turn on the heat and let it boil. Once the mixture boils, simmer it for around 50 minutes. You may add a cup of coconut cream and half of the bagoong alamang after this. Let the mixture cook using low heat until it reduces to a quarter.
  3. Afterwards, we will add the rest of the coconut cream and adjust the flavor by adding more bagoong if necessary.
  4. Make sure to serve this nice and hot
