Dishes

A curated collection of savory meals crafted with passion.

Chicken Adobo

A Filipino comfort dish simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a traditional Filipino dish, composed of pork that has been simmered in coconut milk. This dish has a creamy, savory, and spicy flavor.

Chicken Caldereta

Chicken caldereta is a hearty and flavorful Filipino stew with tender chicken, potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce that is often enhanced with liver spread and cheese.

Pancit Bihon

Stir-fried rice noodles with vegetables and meat, classic Filipino dish.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Creamy, cheesy, and comforting Italian pasta classic.

Sinigang na Fried Bangus

This dish combines the fresh flavor of bangus mixed with the sour and savory taste of the tamarind based broth mixed with fresh vegetables.

Bistek Tagalog

Bistek Tagalog is a type of Filipino beef stew. This is also known as beef steak to some people. It is comprised of thin slices of beef and a generous amount of onions. These are stewed in a soy sauce and lemon juice mixture until the beef gets very tender. It is best enjoyed with warm rice.
