Cassava Cake with Macapuno

Cassava cake with macapuno is a traditional Filipino dessert made of grated cassava, coconut milk, condensed milk and macapuno, which is soft, chewy coconut meat.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen grated cassava
  • Unsalted butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Canned coconut milk
  • Jarred macapuno in syrup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare Pan: Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Grease a baking pan with unsalted butter.
  2. Prepare the Cassava Mixture: In a large bowl, combine: Frozen grated cassava (thawed) Unsalted butter (melted) Brown sugar Sweetened condensed milk Canned coconut milk Mix thoroughly until well combined.
  3. Bake the Cassava Layer: Pour the mixture into the greased pan. Bake for 40â€“50 minutes or until the cassava mixture is firm and lightly golden on top.
  4. Add the Macapuno Topping: Drain the jarred macapuno in syrup. Spread it evenly over the baked cassava layer.
  5. Optional Creamy Top (Optional): If you want a creamier top, mix a little condensed milk and coconut milk and pour over the macapuno before a final bake. Bake for another 10â€“15 minutes until lightly golden.
  6. Cool and Serve: Allow the cake to cool to room temperature. Slice into squares or rectangles and serve.
