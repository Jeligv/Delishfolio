Cassava cake with macapuno is a traditional Filipino dessert made of grated cassava, coconut milk, condensed milk and macapuno, which is soft, chewy coconut meat.

Halo Halo

Halo-halo (also spelled haluhalo) is a popular and vibrant Filipino cold dessert that translates to "mix-mix" in Tagalog. It is essentially a colorful concoction of crushed ice, evaporated milk, and a wide array of sweet ingredients, especially popular as a refreshing treat in hot weather.

Graham Bar

A graham bar typically refers to a no-bake dessert or sweet snack made with layers of graham crackers, a cream mixture (usually all-purpose cream and condensed milk), and various flavorings or toppings like mangoes, ube cheese, or cookies and cream. It is a popular item in the Philippines.
