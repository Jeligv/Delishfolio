Champurado

Sweet chocolate rice porridge, popular breakfast or merienda.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ¬Ω cup sugar
  • Evaporated milk

Instructions:

  1. Boil rice in water until soft.
  2. Add cocoa powder and sugar, stir well.
  3. Serve hot with a drizzle of evaporated milk.
